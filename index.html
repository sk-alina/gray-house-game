The Story

Welcome to the Gray House, a boarding school unlike any other. Here, students with disabilities navigate a world of complex relationships, mysterious traditions, and hidden secrets.

As Sphinx, a new arrival with no arms, you must find your place among the various groups - the Pheasants, Birds, Logs, and others. Each choice you make will determine not only your survival but your identity within these walls.

Climb fire escapes, forge alliances, uncover the truth behind the Sepulcher, and discover what it truly means to belong in a place where graduation might not be what it seems.

Meet the Characters

Sphinx

A newcomer with no arms who must navigate the complex social hierarchy of the Gray House through wit and determination.

Noble

The golden-haired leader with a complex personality, both admired and feared by his peers.

Tabaqui

A wheelchair user with unlimited enthusiasm and philosophical views on mobility and human potential.

Chimera

A mysterious girl with green hair and a sharp tongue, hiding secrets beneath her doll-like appearance.

Smoker

A philosophical observer who sees through the House's illusions and speaks uncomfortable truths.

Jackal

The group's storyteller and commentator, always ready with wit and observations about their strange world.
